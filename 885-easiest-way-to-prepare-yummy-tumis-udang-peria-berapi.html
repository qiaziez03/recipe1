Ingredients of Tumis Udang Peria Berapi

  1. Prepare 250 g of Udang,di kupas.
  2. You need 250 g of Peria,buang biji dan hiris.
  3. You need 3 of Sdb Minyak Canola.
  4. It's 4 Ulas of Bawang putih di cincang halus.
  5. You need 7-10 of bauh Cili api merah.
  6. It's 1/4 of Sdk Garam Himalaya.
  7. You need 1 of Sdk Serbuk Pati Ayam.
  8. You need 1/2 of Sdk Rainbow Peppercorn/Blackpepper.
  9. Prepare 75 ml of Air.

Tumis Udang Peria Berapi step by step

  1. Rendam hirisan Peria dengan air garam selama 15 Minit kemudian Toskan dan bilas hingga bersih..
