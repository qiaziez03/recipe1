Asam pedas (Indonesian and Malay: asam pedas; Minangkabau: asam padeh; English language: sour and spicy) is a Maritime Southeast Asian sour and spicy fish stew dish. Asam pedas believed comes from Minangkabau cuisine of West Sumatra. Asam pedas yang sedap, pastinya kuah pekat dan sedap secukup rasa masam dan pedasnya.
