Ingredients of Tomyam Simple

  4. You need 2 batang of serai (potong serong).

  6. It's 5 helai of daun limau.
  7. You need 1 of sb sos ikan.
  8. You need 200 gm of sotong (bersihkan).
  9. It's 200 gm of udang (bersihkan).
  10. It's 1 batang of lobak merah (potong kecil).
  11. You need 200 gm of bunga kobis.
  12. You need 2 of biji tomato (belah 2).
  13. Prepare 100 gm of cendawan (carik halus).
