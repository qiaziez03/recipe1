Ingredients of Teh Tarik Masala Kaww

  1. Prepare 3 of sdbTeh (apa - apa jenama).
  2. You need 2 cawan of Air.
  3. It's 1/4-1/2 cawan of susu pekat manis (ejas ikut citarasa).
  4. It's 1/2 of kulit Kayu manis (kali ni tak bubuh).
  5. Prepare 3 of biji Buah pelaga.
  6. Prepare of Secubit garam.

Teh Tarik Masala Kaww step by step

  1. Panaskan air, masukkan serbuk teh, masak sampai mendidih. Kemudian tuangkan susu pekat. Kacau sebati. Biar mendidih sekali lagi..
  2. Masukkan garam dan biji pelaga. Kacau rata..
