Ingredients of Char Koew Tiew Ayam

  1. Prepare 500 gm of isi ayam.
  2. Prepare 1.5 kg of peket Koew Tiew.
  3. You need 10 ulas of bawang putih.
  4. It's 3 of sudu kicap pekat.
  5. It's 3 of sudu kicap manis.
  6. It's 1 of sudu besar gula.
  7. You need 1 sudu teh of garam.
  8. Prepare of telur 2 biji.
  9. You need 1 sudu teh of serbuk belacan.
  10. Prepare of cili boh 5 sudu besar,wie guna minyak sambal je.
  11. It's of sawi secekak(tak letak tak ada).
  12. It's 2 cawan of taugeh.
