Ingredients of Ayam Kicap Pedas

  1. It's of Ayam yg telah digoreng.
  2. Prepare of Bawang Merah.
  3. You need of Bawang Putih.
  4. You need of Halia.
  5. Prepare of Cili Padi - Pedas ikut suka.
  6. It's Daun of Kari.
  7. You need of Sos cili.
  8. It's of Sos tiram.
  9. It's of Kicap pekat - untuk bagi warna.

Today we're making a Spicy Soy Sauce Chicken stirfry, also known as Ayam Masak Kicap Pedas. This is another traditional Malay dish and this is my Mom's recipe. It's one of my favourite dishes ever to have over rice. The base flavour is Kicap Manis, of Dark Sweet Soya Sauce.

Ayam Kicap Pedas instructions

  1. Blend bawang merah, putih, halia & cili padi.
  2. Tumiskankan bahan blend.
  3. Masukkan daun kari.
  4. Bila dah garing masukkan segala sos, jangan letak kicap pekat terlalu banyak, hanyan untuk bagi warna sahaja.
  5. Kacau2 sekejap, masukkan air, letak ikut suka kuah banyak mana.
  6. Biarkan kuah agak sedikit likat.
  7. Masukkan ayam dan balancekan rasa dengan sedikit garam.
