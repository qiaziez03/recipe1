  3. It's 1 1/2 cawan of beras.
  4. Prepare 1 cawan of santan kelapa.
  5. Prepare 5 of biji bawang merah (hiris halus).
  6. It's 1 of Inci halia (hiris halus).

Nasi Lemak Lemuni instructions

  1. Daun lemuni dikisar dan ditapis airnya..
