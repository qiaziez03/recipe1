Ingredients of Mee Goreng Basah Sempoi

  1. It's 1 of peket mee kuning.
  2. You need 15 of ekor udang.
  3. It's 1 of ikat sawi.
  4. You need 2 of biji telur.
  5. Prepare 3 ulas of bawang putih.
  6. You need 1 of biji bawang holland (hiris kasar).
  7. Prepare 1-2 of tangkai cili merah (potong serong).
  8. It's 1 of sdb cili kisar.
  9. You need 3 of sdb sos cili.
  10. You need 2 of sdb kicap.
  11. Prepare 1 of sdt garam (jika perlu).
  12. You need 1-2 of sdb gula.

Mee Goreng means fried noodles in Malaysian language. In Malaysia, you can find this noodle dish in the market, or by the roadside. Many Indians in Malaysia are Muslim, and they are called Mamak locally. Indian-style fried yellow noodles are commonly found in Malaysia.

Mee Goreng Basah Sempoi instructions

  1. Tumis bawang putih dan bawang holland sehingga wangi. Masukkan cili kisar. Masak sampai pecah minyak..
  2. Masukkan sos cili dan kicap serta gula secukupnya. Tambah garam jika perlu. Masukkan pula udang dan air rebusan udang..
  3. Masukkan mee, gaul sebati..
  4. Masukkan telur.Kacau sebati..
  5. Akhir sekali masukkan sawi. Teruskan menggoreng sampai mee & sayur masak sempurna. Padamkan api..
  6. Sedia untuk dihidang..
