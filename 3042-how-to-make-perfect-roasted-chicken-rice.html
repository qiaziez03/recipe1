Roasted Chicken Rice instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with all ingredients (A) for at least 10 mins.
  2. Lightly pan-fry chicken for 3 mins or until chicken turns light brown. Place chicken on baking tray and bake in the preheat oven at 180 degrees for 20 - 30 mins.
  3. Mixed all ingredients (B) and bring to a boil.
  4. Mixed ingredients (C) in washed rice and cook in a rice cooker. If you are not using the concentrated chicken stock, use the chicken soup to cook the rice.
  5. Blend all ingredients (D) and cook for about 2 mins if you like.
  6. Plate the chicken and garnish with lettuce and tomatoes.
