Ingredients of Mee Goreng Basah Sempoi

  1. Prepare of Bahan utama.
  2. It's 3 ulas of bawang putih.
  3. Prepare 1 keping of isi ayam di potong hiris.
  4. It's 200 gram of mee kuning.
  5. Prepare 3-4 helai of sayur sawi.
  6. It's 2 of biji telur.
  7. You need of Bahan Sos.
  8. You need 2 of sudu kicap manis.
  9. Prepare 2 of sudu sos cili.

Mee Goreng means fried noodles in Malaysian language. In Malaysia, you can find this noodle dish in the market, or by the roadside. Many Indians in Malaysia are Muslim, and they are called Mamak locally. Indian-style fried yellow noodles are commonly found in Malaysia.
