Ayam 3 Budak Gemok instructions

  1. Marinate chicken pieces with turmeric powder..
  2. Peel the garlic, holland onions and blend them together..
  3. Stir fry chicken using cooking oil. Lift up the chicken and set aside. Left a little cooking oil for the next step..
  4. Prepare curry leaves. Stir fry blended mixture and put in holland onions and curry leaves into the cooker..
  5. Add in the chicken into cooker. Then, add soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar and fine salt necessarily. Cooker another 15 minutes. Meal is ready to serve..
