Ingredients of Ikan Goreng Sambal Nenas

  1. You need of Ikan kembung 7-10 ekor (terpulang nak guna jenis ikan).
  2. Prepare 1 potong of nenas (dipotong dadu).
  3. It's 1 of biji bawang merah ditumbuk.
  4. Prepare 2 ulas of bawang putih ditumbuk.
  5. It's 1/2 of inci halia ditumbuk.
  6. Prepare 1 batang of serai diketuk.
  7. Prepare 1 of senduk cili yang sudah dimesen.
  8. It's 2 of sudu sos tiram.
  9. You need 1/2 of sudu garam.
  10. It's 1/2 of sudu perasa masakan hilal.
  11. You need 1/4 of sudu gula (blh ganti dengan gula melaka).
  12. You need 1/2 cawan of air.
