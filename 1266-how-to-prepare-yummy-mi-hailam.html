Ingredients of Mi Hailam

  1. You need of Bahan Kuah.
  2. You need 300 kg of daging - hiris kemudian rebus.
  3. Prepare 1 keping of dada ayam - potong kecil-kecil.
  4. It's 300 g of udang.
  5. Prepare 1 of inci halia (ketuk).
  6. You need 3 ulas of bawang putih (ketuk @ cincang halus).
  7. Prepare 10 of biji cili padi (ketuk sikit).
  8. It's 6-7 of sudu besar cili kisar (nak pedas boleh tambah lagi).
  9. Prepare 1 botol of kecil sos tiram (MAGGI).
  10. It's 70 g of kicap pekat (JALEN WARNA UNGU).
  11. You need 70 g of sos cili (MAGGI).
  12. Prepare 6 of sudu besar tepung jagung.
  13. You need of Bahan pelengkap.
  14. It's 2 of paket mi kuning (celur).
  15. You need 1 of pek fish cake (celur).
  16. Prepare 1 of pek fish ball (celur).
  17. You need 1 of cekak sawi (potong & celur).
  18. It's 3 of biji cili merah (hiris).
