Ingredients of Tom Yum Putih

  1. It's 1/2 of dada ayam (di potong kecil).
  2. Prepare 250 gram of daging (dihiris nipis).
  3. It's 1 of labu bawang besar (di potong).
  4. It's 2 ulas of bawang putih (ditumbuk).
  5. It's 1/2 inchi of halia (dihiris nipis).
  6. You need 5 of biji cili padi.
  7. Prepare 2 batang of serai (dititik).
  8. Prepare 1 of tangkai daun limau purut.
  9. Prepare 1 of biji asam limau purut (diambil airnya).
  10. It's 2 of biji tomato (dibelah 4).
  11. It's 1 of sudu kecil cuka(optional).
