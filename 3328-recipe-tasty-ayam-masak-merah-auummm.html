Ayam Masak Merah is one of my favourite curries ever! Yep, my tastes are totally South East Asian; as far as I'm concerned, it's hard to beat a good Malay or Indonesian curry! 'Ayam masak merah' literally means chicken cooked in red sauce. The red sauce mentioned here comes from tomatoes and is mildly spiced with aromatics such as ginger, garlic and dried chillies. The chicken pieces are marinated with turmeric powder and deep fried before being braised in the spiced.

Ayam Masak Merah Auummm step by step

  1. Panaskan minyak..banyak sikit takpe sbb kita nak masakkan sambal. Then tumis bahan blend dan 3 sekawan sampai wangi..
  2. Then masukkan cili giling. Masakkan sampai tukar kaler gelap. Pakai api perlahan so tak hangit sambal. Dan rajin2 kacau..
  3. Then masukkan semua sauce serta garam. Rasakan..mana rasa kurang, tambah la apa2 yg patut..hiks.
  4. Then, masukkan bawang dan ayam goreng separuh tu..then masakkan nya. Tutup lid..biarkan jap..then bukak lid and gaul2 sampai masak dan sebati..
